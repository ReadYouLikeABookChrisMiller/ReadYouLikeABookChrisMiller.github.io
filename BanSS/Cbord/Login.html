Manage Your Money, View Your Balances & More!

This site allows you to add funds to your RHIT campus ID card using Visa, Mastercard, Discover or American Express debit/credit cards.  These deposited funds can be used for a variety of dining or laundry services on campus.  This site also allows you to check your transaction history, view account balances and report your card lost or found.

If you have any questions or concerns about adding funds to your card, please contact Student Financial Services by email at GETFUNDS@rose-hulman.edu or by phone at (812) 877-8296. 

To purchase a student meal plan, contact Student Affairs by email at rhodeska@rose-hulman.edu or by phone at (812) 877-8651.
